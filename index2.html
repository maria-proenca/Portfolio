Maria Proença (b. 2001, Lisbon) is a communication and graphic designer with a focus (but not limited to) on editorial design and visual identity, weaving discourses across both print and digital media. Seeing communication design as a lens to explore, question, and make sense of the world, she aspires to make meaning move through multidisciplinary projects within the social, cultural, and artistic fields.
